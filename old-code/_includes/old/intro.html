The Best Formula One Team

Lenny Martinez Dominguez

In the 69-year history of the sport, over 800 men and women have raced to win the Formula One Driver's Cup. Only 33 drivers have held it.

These 33 drivers have all won while racing for the same 16 teams (by descending number of championship wins): Ferrari, McLaren, Williams, Mercedes, Lotus, Red Bull, Brabham, Renault, Cooper, Benetton, Tyrrell, Alfa Romeo, BRM, Matra, and Brawn GP.

But which team has had the best championship season? How do we define best?
